## What is MetriQ?

MetriQ is where raw data from the plant floor becomes a screen that helps you
decide. Dozens — or thousands — of devices (pumps, valves, boilers,
compressors) pulse in a single live facility view; anomalies turn into alarms
instantly; and AI condenses the noise into readable reports and analyses.

The goal is simple: **not just to watch data, but to understand it.**

## Phase‑1 — Coming Soon

The first phase delivers the core backbone of the platform:

-  **Modbus data acquisition** — Reliable, scheduled polling of registers from
  Modbus devices in the field.
-  **High-performance storage** — Efficient, scalable retention of
  high-frequency telemetry.
-  **AI Copilot — smart reports & analyses** — A Copilot-style AI that
  generates automatic status summaries, analyses and reports from live data.
-  **Alarms** — Warning/critical alarms on threshold breaches, with an
  acknowledge flow and notifications.
-  **Reports** — Shareable reports scoped to the whole facility or a single
  area.

> Additional protocols and more advanced analytics are on the roadmap for later
> phases.
